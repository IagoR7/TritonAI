Simulation notes
• Each tag generates daily pings for the past 60 days.
• Each ping includes SST, Chlorophyll‑a, Sea Surface Height (SSH), depth and a computed feeding score.
• An accelerometer spike may occur (speed burst → likely hunting). Some spikes include a diet event (what the shark ate).
• Positions are constrained to the ocean side of the coastline model to avoid inland hotspots.
